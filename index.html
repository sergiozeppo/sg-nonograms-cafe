RosimInc's Nonogram Café

Instructions

  • Fill squares so that groups of squares match the hints of that row/column
  • Groups must be separated by one or more empty squares
  • Once you fill all the cells correctly, the playing field will turn green, and below it you will see a secret message.

  • Click to fill a cell
  • Right-click to mark with an X that it cannot be filled
  • Click or right-click again to remove the marking
  • Click on a hint to toggle if it's grayed out
  • Dragging is enabled
  • Touch controls for mobile also enabled
  • Ctrl+Z or left arrow to undo
  • Ctrl+Y or right arrow to redo
  • + key or up arrow to zoom in
  • - key or down arrow to zoom out
  • Shift+R to reset puzzle progress (this cannot be undone)
  • Shift+Q to clear all cache (this will reset all puzzles)
  • Progress will be kept between sessions (but not the undo/redo history)
